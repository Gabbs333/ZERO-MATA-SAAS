# Product Overview

## Snack Bar Management System

A digital solution designed to prevent stock theft in Cameroonian snack bars by creating a traceable chain from customer order to product delivery. The system ensures all sales go through official stock channels and provides real-time visibility to owners.

## Core Problem Solved

Eliminates unauthorized sales of products outside the official inventory by requiring all transactions to flow through a centralized validation system at the counter.

## Key Features

- Digital order taking via mobile app (waitresses)
- Centralized order validation at counter
- Real-time stock tracking and alerts
- Supply chain management (ravitaillements)
- Invoice generation and payment tracking
- Analytics dashboard for owners
- Complete sales traceability and audit logs
- Role-based access control (waitress, counter, manager, owner)

## Business Model

The system tracks:
- Revenue (validated orders)
- Cash flow (actual payments received)
- Outstanding receivables (unpaid invoices)
- Profit margins (revenue minus supply costs)
- Stock movements (entries and exits)
